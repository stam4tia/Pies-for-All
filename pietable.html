Pie Table

What will you bring to the table?


~Sweet Pies~

Apple Pie: The first apple pie recipe was printed in England in 1381.

Pumpkin Pie: Pie with a spiced, pumpkin-based custard filling, a symbol of harvest time.

Cherry Pie: A pie meant for royal tastes, was baked first for Queen Elizabeth I.

Pecan Pie: Pecan pie originates from the southern United States.

Key Lime Pie: In 1965, Florida named key lime pie its official state pie.

-Savory Pies-

Chicken Pot Pie: It began as a recipe used to stretch the week's leftover meat

Shepherd's Pie: Got its name because it's usually made of lamb, which shepherds herd.

Quiche Lorraine: Originally a meal enjoyed by peasants who needed a hearty meal.

Spanakopita: AKA spinach pie, which has existed within Greek cuisine for a long time.

Pizza Pie: The United States eats 350 slices of pizza every second.
